## Core Features

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-shield-lock: **Total Privacy**
    
    ---
    
    Runs completely offline on your local machine. Your sensitive documents never leave your computer.

-   :material-brain: **Local LLMs**
    
    ---
    
    Powered by state-of-the-art local models like Qwen 2.5 and Qwen 3 (via Ollama) for high-quality, nuanced translations.

-   :material-file-document-multiple: **File Format Support**
    
    ---
    
    Translates PDF, Word, Markdown, Excel, CSV, and even Images (via OCR), exporting back to the original format.

-   :material-translate: **Multi-Lingual**
    
    ---
    
    Optimized for English, Chinese, German, and French. Auto-detects input languages seamlessly.

-   :material-chart-bell-curve-cumulative: **Dashboard & Analytics**
    
    ---
    
    Tracks your translation history, measures processing speed (tokens/sec), and provides detailed reporting.

-   :material-palette: **Glassmorphism UI**
    
    ---
    
    A stunning, modern interface built with Vue 3, featuring fluid animations and full Dark Mode support.

</div>
